Projects

The following are the main research topics I’m currently working on.

  • Rationality as Truth Approximation. The project explores the role of the philosophical notion of “closeness to the truth” (truthlikeness or verisimilitude) in scientific and ordinary reasoning. Main activities and sub-projects:
    • From models to decisions (PRIN 2017 project) (with Jan Sprenger, Giovanni Valente, and Carlo Martini)
    • Experimental Cognitive Decision Theory
  • Reasoning, uncertainty, and expert judgment
    • Uncertainty and fallacies in legal reasoning
    • Decisions, democracy and expert judgment
    • NUTS
    • Rationality, game theory, and human science
